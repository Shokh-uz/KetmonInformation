1. Problem and Solution

Problem: Fragmented & Opaque Travel in Uzbekistan

Tourists face significant challenges in Uzbekistan, navigating disparate information and inconsistent offerings.

Price & Transparency Issues

  • Difficulty comparing tour packages
  • Widely varying prices
  • Lack of transparency

Low Digitalization

  • Many agencies lack online presence
  • Inefficient customer acquisition
  • Operational bottlenecks

No Centralized Platform

  • No unified online platform for tours
  • Difficult to compare options
  • No secure online payment system

Solution: KETMON - Your Gateway to Seamless Travel

KETMON transforms Central Asian tourism with a comprehensive platform, solving key travel challenges for both tourists and agencies.

Aggregated Tour Marketplace

KETMON is a dynamic marketplace for tours:

  • Brings diverse packages from multiple agencies
  • Offers a single, intuitive platform

Effortless Comparison & Booking

Compare and book with ease:

  • Filter by date, price, destination, reviews, and availability
  • Integrated secure online payments
  • Frictionless booking experience

Flexible Payment Options

Travel is more accessible with:

  • Optional installment systems
  • Branded as Travel Now Pay Later (TNPL)

Intelligent Recommendations

Find your perfect adventure:

  • AI-powered tour suggestions
  • Personalized based on preferences, past searches, and trends

Expected Impact

KETMON addresses the efficiency and social effect challenges in Uzbekistan's tourism sector by:

  • Creating transparency in pricing and tour offerings
  • Empowering local tour agencies with digital tools and broader market reach
  • Reducing time and effort for tourists to find and book quality tours
  • Promoting sustainable and ethical tourism practices
  • Contributing to regional economic growth and job creation
