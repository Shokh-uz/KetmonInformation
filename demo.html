Demo Video Description

In this demo video, we present the initial working version — MVP — of our new web platform designed for travel services.

Platform Features

The platform features a modern, intuitive interface designed to provide maximum convenience for clients. The top panel of the page includes main navigation sections — Home, Destinations, About Us, Contact — as well as language and currency selection functions.

Search System

The central section features a search panel created for users, offering not only text search but also voice search capabilities. This solution is based on the principles of modernity and user convenience of the platform.

Popular Destinations

The video focuses primarily on the Popular Destinations section. Here, popular cities such as Dubai, Paris, and Istanbul are presented in card format with high-quality images, brief descriptions, and special labels (Popular, Discount, New).

MVP Goal

The main goal of the MVP version is to demonstrate the platform's visual design, page structure, and core functions in practice. In the final version, features such as the booking process, price comparison, reviews, and additional services will be further expanded.

Design Concept

The platform design is minimalist, based on pastel colors, and fully complies with modern UX/UI standards for the tourism services market. The demo video clearly reflects the project's concept, value, and conveniences provided to users.

User Convenience

Each destination is displayed in card format, making it easy for clients to quickly make choices, compare options, and proceed to the next step.
